📄 SoK: Understanding zk-SNARKs: The Gap Between Research and Practice
- https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.02387

💻 hash-circuits
- https://github.com/bkomuves/hash-circuits
- Balazs Komuves发布了hash-circuits开源项目，包含多种流行哈希函数的circom实现，如SHA2、Keccak/SHA3、Blake2、Poseidon2、Griffin和MiMC，并提供了约束数量比较。
